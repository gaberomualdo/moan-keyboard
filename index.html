Download Instructions

1. Click download button and get the MoanKeyboard.zip file.

2. Unzip the MoanKeyboard.zip file — it should be in your downloads folder.

3. Open Google Chrome and click on the menu (3 dots stacked) icon in the top right corner, then click on More Tools > Extensions.

4. Turn on Developer Mode on the top right corner by sliding the slider right, or clicking on the slider.

5. Go to the top left hand corner and click the Load Unpacked button, then click on your downloads folder, then click on the MoanKeyboard folder, then click select on the bottom right corner of the file box.

You're done!
